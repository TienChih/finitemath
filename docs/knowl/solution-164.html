Solution 8.3.13.1.

Let \(W\) denote “withdraw cash” and \(B\) denote “Check Balance”. We are then given \(P(W)=0.92, P(B)=0.32, P(W\cup B)=0.96\text{.}\) Notice then that:

\begin{align*} P(W\cup B)\amp=P(W)+P(B)-P(W\cap B)\\ 0.96\amp=0.92+0.32-P(W\cap B)\\ P(W\cap B)\amp=0.28 \end{align*}

Which let's us fill in the following diagram:

We are tasked with finding \(P(W|B)\text{.}\) Note that \(P(W|B)=\frac{P(W\cap B)}{P(B)}=\frac{0.28}{0.32}=0.875\) or 87.5%.

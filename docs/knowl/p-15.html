The purpose of these interactives go beyond simplifying the course. Mathematics is best learned, not through passive absorption, but active engagement. Thus, the author encourages the reader to modify, play with and interact with the interactives. The readers and encouraged to modify the interactives to suit other problems encountered in the text, or to save the interactives themselves so they can be “messed with” by the reader at their leisure. This is the best way to use this text.

We have that there is a 100% chance Abed walks on Monday, so the respective probability of his modes of transport 4 days later are:

\begin{equation*} \begin{pmatrix}B_4 \\ W_4 \end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix} 0.2 \amp 0.5 \\ 0.8 \amp 0.5\end{pmatrix}^{4}\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}= \begin{pmatrix} 0.3896 \amp 0.3815 \\ 0.6104 \amp 0.6185\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}= \begin{pmatrix} 0.3896 \\ 0.6104 \end{pmatrix}. \end{equation*}

So there is a 38.96% chance he will take the bus ( and a 61.04% chance he will walk).

Solution 7.2.3.1.

We can use either the Desmos calculator above, or algebra:

  • The average rate of change of \(R(t)\) over the interval \([1,3]\) is
    \begin{align*} \frac{R(3)-R(1)}{3-1}\amp = \frac{60-40e^{-0.05(3)}-(60-40e^{-0.05(1)})}{2}\\ \amp = \frac{-40e^{-0.05(3)}+40e^{-0.05(1)}}{2}\\ \amp\approx 1.81042896. \end{align*}
    So the average rate of change in the weekly revenue between 1 and 3 weeks is about \(\$1810.43\text{.}\)
  • The average rate of change of \(R(t)\) over the interval \([2,2.5]\) is
    \begin{align*} \frac{R(2.5)-R(2)}{2.5-2}\amp = \frac{60-40e^{-0.05(2.5)}-(60-40e^{-0.05(2)})}{0.5}\\ \amp = \frac{-40e^{-0.05(2.5)}+40e^{-0.05(2)}}{0.5}\\ \amp\approx 1.78724123611. \end{align*}
    So the average rate of change in the weekly revenue between 2 and 2.5 weeks is about \(\$1787.24\text{.}\)

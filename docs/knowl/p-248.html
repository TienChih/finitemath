If the cat's heart weighs 13 g, then we have \(y=13\text{.}\) So we have:

\begin{align*} y\amp= 3.85777x+0.234354\\ 13\amp= 3.85777x+0.234354\\ 3.85777x\amp=13-0.234354\\ 3.85777x\amp=12.765646\\ x\amp=\frac{12.765646}{3.85777}\approx 3.309 \end{align*}

So a cat whose heart weighs 13 g most likely weighs about 3.309 kg.

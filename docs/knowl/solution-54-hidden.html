If we had 140 quarts of pineapple juice, 440 quarts of orange juice and 140 quarts of kiwi juice, the amount of product we could make would be:

\begin{equation*} \begin{pmatrix} \frac{1}{4}\amp \frac{1}{4} \amp -\frac{3}{4} \\ \frac{1}{6}\amp -\frac{1}{6} \amp \frac{1}{2} \\ -\frac{1}{6}\amp \frac{1}{6} \amp \frac{1}{2}\end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} 140\\440\\140 \end{pmatrix}= \begin{pmatrix}40 \\ 20\\120\end{pmatrix} \end{equation*}

That is, 40 gallons of PineOrange, 20 gallons of PineKiwi and 120 gallons of OrangeKiwi.

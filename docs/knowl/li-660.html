Item 2.
Let \(B\) denote “all black cards”, and \(N\) denote “all non-face cards”. Note \(P(B)=\frac{C(26,5)}{C(52,5)}\text{,}\) \(P(N)=\frac{C(36,5)}{C(52,5)}\text{,}\) and \(P(B\cap N)=\frac{C(18,5)}{C(52,5)}\text{.}\) Thus
\begin{equation*} P(B\cup N)=P(B)+P(N)-P(B\cap N)=\frac{C(26,5)+C(36,5)-C(18,5)}{C(52,5)}=\frac{65780+376992-8568}{2598960}\approx 0.167068. \end{equation*}

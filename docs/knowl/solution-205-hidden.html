The chance you win nothing would be \(1-\frac{1}{10}-\frac{3}{10}=\frac{6}{10}\text{.}\) So the probability distribution would be:

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{|c|ccc|} \hline x \amp 0 \amp 5 \amp 20 \\ \hline P(X=x) \amp \frac{6}{10} \amp \frac{3}{10} \amp \frac{1}{10} \\ \hline \end{array} \end{equation*}

There is a \(\frac{3}{10}+\frac{1}{10}\) chance you win something.

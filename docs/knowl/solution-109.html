Solution 8.2.8.1.

If we let \(B\) denote people who ordered burgers and \(H\) denote people who ordered hotdogs, we have:

\begin{equation*} |B|=18, |H|=6, |B\cup H|=20, \end{equation*}

thus

\begin{align*} |B\cup H|\amp=|B|+|H|-|B\cap H|\\ 20\amp=18+6-|B\cap H|\\ |B\cap H|\amp=4. \end{align*}

So four people ordered both.

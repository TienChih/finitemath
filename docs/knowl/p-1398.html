We note that in a year, city \(X\) will keep 60% of it's current population \(X_0=1000\) and attain 20% of \(Y's\) population, so after one year:

\begin{equation*} X_1=0.6\cdot X_0+0.2\cdot Y_0=0.6\cdot 1000+0.2\cdot 1000=600+200=800. \end{equation*}

Similarly, \(Y\) attains 40% of \(X's\) population and retains 80% of it's own, so after one year:

\begin{equation*} Y_1=0.4\cdot X_0+0.8\cdot Y_0=0.4\cdot 1000+0.8\cdot 1000=400+800=1200. \end{equation*}

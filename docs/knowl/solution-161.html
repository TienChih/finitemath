Solution 9.5.7.1.

Recall that \(n=8, p=0.25.\) Let \(X\) denote the number of policies that pay out.

  1. This is \(P(X=3)=C(8,3)(0.25)^3(0.75)^5=0.2076\text{.}\)
  2. This is the sum of one through eight policies paying out, but its also just removing the case where no policies paid out. So
    \begin{equation*} 1-P(X=0)=1-C(8,0)(0.25)^0(0.75)^8\approx 1-0.1001=0.8999. \end{equation*}
  3. This is \(E(X)=np=8(0.25)=2\) policies.

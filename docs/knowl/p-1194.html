There are \(C(20,3)\) ways to pick a commttee of 3, \(C(20,4)\) ways to pick a commttee of 4 and \(C(20,5)\) ways to pick a commttee of 5. So the total number of ways to pick commttees is

\begin{equation*} C(20,3)+C(20,4)+C(20,5)=1140+4845+15504=21489. \end{equation*}

Note that there are no overlaps between these selections, they may share people, but there is no 3 person committee who is also a 4 person committee etc.

Item 9.2.9.5.
This is one, two or three vegetarian dishes, so
\begin{equation*} C(4,1)\cdot C(6,2)+C(4,2)\cdot C(6,1)+C(4,3)\cdot C(6,0)=4\cdot15 + 6\cdot 6+4\cdot 1 =100 \end{equation*}
ways to have at least one vegetarian dish. Or if we took all 120 caterung options, and removed the 20 without vegetarian options, we would have \(120-20=100\) dishes.

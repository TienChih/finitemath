A company sells widgets for \(\$50\) each. Unfortunately, no one wants to buy them. The discover that for each dollar they lower the price, 5 more people are willing to buy a widget. The marginal cost of producing a widget is \(\$10\) with fixed cost \(\$300\text{.}\) How should the widget be priced to maximize profit? What is this profit, and how many widgets would be sold?

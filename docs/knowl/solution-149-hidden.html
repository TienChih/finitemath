There are \(8^5\) ways the passangers could get off the elevator. The probability that everyone gets off at different floors is \(\frac{P(8,5)}{8^5}=\frac{6720}{32768}\approx0.2051\text{.}\) So the probability that this doesn't happen, that is, at least two get off at the same floor, is:

\begin{equation*} 1-\frac{P(8,5)}{8^5}=1-\frac{6720}{32768}\approx0.7949. \end{equation*}

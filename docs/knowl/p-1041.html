The derivative of \(f(x)=x^2\) is:

\begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx}[x^2]\amp = \limit{h}{0}\frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h}\\ \amp = \limit{h}{0} \frac{(x+h)^2-x^2}{h}\\ \amp = \limit{h}{0} \frac{x^2+2xh+h^2-x^2}{h}\\ \amp = \limit{h}{0} \frac{(2x+h)h}{h}\\ \amp = \limit{h}{0} 2x+h=2x\text{.} \end{align*}

We note that \(G'(x)=\frac{d}{dx}[-0.2x^2 +450]=-0.4x \text{.}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle G(10)=-0.2(10)^2 +450=430.\)
  2. \(\displaystyle G(25)=-0.2(25)^2 +450=325.\)
  3. \(\displaystyle G'(10)=-0.4(10)=-4.\)
  4. \(\displaystyle G'(25)=-0.4(25) =-10.\)

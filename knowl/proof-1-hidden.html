Consider the following truth tables:
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|c|c|c} p\amp q\amp p\vee q \amp \sim(p\vee q)\\ \hline T \amp T\amp T \amp F\\ T \amp F\amp T \amp F\\ F \amp T\amp T \amp F\\ F \amp F\amp F \amp T\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|c|c|c|c} p\amp q\amp \sim p \amp \sim q \amp \sim p \wedge \sim q\\ \hline T \amp T\amp F \amp F\amp F\\ T \amp F\amp F \amp T\amp F\\ F \amp T\amp T \amp F\amp F\\ F \amp F\amp T \amp T\amp T\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
Thus \(\sim(p \vee q)=\sim p \wedge \sim q\text{.}\) Similarly:
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|c|c|c} p\amp q\amp p\wedge q \amp \sim(p\wedge q)\\ \hline T \amp T\amp T \amp F\\ T \amp F\amp F \amp T\\ F \amp T\amp F \amp T\\ F \amp F\amp F \amp T\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|c|c|c|c} p\amp q\amp \sim p \amp \sim q \amp \sim p \vee \sim q\\ \hline T \amp T\amp F \amp F\amp F\\ T \amp F\amp F \amp T\amp T\\ F \amp T\amp T \amp F\amp T\\ F \amp F\amp T \amp T\amp T\\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
Thus \(\sim(p \wedge q)=\sim p \vee \sim q\text{.}\)
